Since 1912

More Than a Biscuit.
A Legend.

Born in New York City in 1912, the OREO cookie has grown into the world's best-selling cookie — available in over 100 countries and enjoyed by millions every single day. What started as two chocolate wafers with a cream filling has evolved into a universe of over 85 flavors and counting.

Today, OREO produces more than 40 billion cookies per year, each one carrying the same iconic embossed design that has stayed almost unchanged since 1952. Every twist reveals not just a cookie — but a moment of pure joy.

The OREO Way

The Sacred Ritual

01
🍪

Twist

The legendary move that starts it all. Gently twist the two wafers apart to reveal the pristine crème inside. Everyone has their own technique — and every technique is exactly right.

02
👅

Lick

The moment of pure bliss. Savor the sweet crème — vanilla, mint, peanut butter, or birthday cake. This is the heart of the cookie, the soul of the OREO experience.

03
🥛

Dunk

The grand finale. Submerge in cold milk for exactly the right number of seconds — then bring it to your lips before it crumbles. Pure perfection. No science can explain why it's so good.
