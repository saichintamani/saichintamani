# MediReach AI

**Description:** A multi-agent rural healthcare assistant built for an "Agents for Good" track. Designed to help underserved rural populations access healthcare guidance, locate facilities, get first-aid steps, identify medicines, and find government health schemes — all through coordinated AI agents.

**Architecture — Four specialized agents:**
- 🗺️ **Locator Agent** — finds nearby healthcare facilities
- 🩹 **First-Aid Agent** — provides immediate-response guidance
- 💊 **Medicine Agent** — helps identify and explain medicines
- 📋 **Scheme Agent** — surfaces relevant government health schemes

**Tech Stack:** React, Next.js, Google Gemini (agent intelligence), built using Google's Antigravity agentic IDE.

**Status:** Capstone project, actively developed.
